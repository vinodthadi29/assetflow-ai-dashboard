## RECORDING TIPS

### Pro Tips
- Record mouse movements slowly and deliberately
- Click clearly and deliberately
- Don't talk during screen recording (add voice-over later)
- Record each segment separately (easier to edit)
- Record 2-3 takes of each segment (keep best)
- Have demo data pre-created (no creation during recording)
- Pause 2-3 seconds at key moments
- Use cursor highlights to emphasize important UI
- Show error handling gracefully

### Don'ts
- Don't record too fast (hard to follow)
- Don't include dead time or loading screens
- Don't talk while recording (voice-over added later)
- Don't show mistakes or failures
- Don't use placeholder data
- Don't have jump cuts or abrupt transitions

## AUDIO/VOICE-OVER GUIDELINES

### Voice-Over Style
- Professional and confident tone
- Enterprise-focused language
- No filler words ("um", "uh", "like")
- Conversational pace (not robotic)
- Emphasize business value, not technical details
- Speak clearly and distinctly
- Pause for dramatic effect at key moments

### Voice-Over Framework
```
Segment Formula:
1. Describe what the feature DOES (outcome)
2. Show how it works (visual)
3. Explain the business IMPACT (ROI)
4. Transition to next feature
```

### Example Good Voice-Over
"Asset management in AssetFlow combines power with simplicity.
Register new assets, upload photos, generate QR codes.
Everything is tracked automatically.

Search for any asset across the entire enterprise.
View complete history and current status.
Perfect for audits, compliance, and accountability."

### Example Bad Voice-Over
"This page shows an asset list with sorting and filtering enabled.
You can click to view details and search for specific items.
There's a dropdown menu for export options."

## EXPORT SETTINGS

### Required Specifications
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1920×1080 (minimum)
- **Frame Rate**: 60 FPS
- **Bitrate**: 8-15 Mbps (high quality)
- **Audio**: Stereo, 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
- **Audio Bitrate**: 320 kbps
- **Color Space**: Rec. 709
- **File Size**: <500MB

### Export Command (Example)
```bash
# Using ffmpeg
ffmpeg -i input.mov \
  -c:v libx264 -preset medium -crf 18 \
  -c:a aac -b:a 320k \
  -s 1920x1080 -r 60 \
  AssetFlow_Demo_Final.mp4
```

## MUSIC & SOUND EFFECTS

### Background Music (Recommended)
- **Style**: Modern corporate, inspiring, minimal
- **Duration**: 4:00-5:00 minutes
- **Volume**: 20-30% during voice-over, 70% during silent moments
- **Sources**:
  - AudioJungle (paid royalty-free)
  - Epidemic Sound (subscription)
  - YouTube Audio Library (free)
  - Artlist (subscription)

### Sound Effects
- **Button click**: Soft, responsive (20-30ms)
- **Success notification**: Satisfying chime (0.5s)
- **Conflict warning**: Alert sound (0.3s)
- **Loading**: Subtle, non-intrusive (1-2s)
- **Transition whoosh**: Smooth, not jarring (0.5s)

### Audio Mixing
- Voice-over: -6dB to -3dB (primary)
- Background music: -18dB to -15dB (background)
- Sound effects: -9dB to -6dB (emphasis)
- Music + SFX + VO: -20dB total (leave headroom)

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

❌ **Rushing the video**
→ Slow down. Let features breathe. No dead time.

❌ **Using placeholder data**
→ Use realistic enterprise data (247 assets, real names, etc.)

❌ **Poor audio quality**
→ Invest in a decent USB microphone (~$50-100)

❌ **Showing errors or failures**
→ Only show successful paths and graceful error handling

❌ **Too much jargon**
→ Focus on business value, not technical implementation

❌ **Abrupt transitions**
→ Use smooth 0.5-second transitions between segments

❌ **Bad lighting or environment**
→ Record in a quiet, well-lit space

❌ **Unclear voice-over**
→ Record multiple takes. Use professional microphone.
